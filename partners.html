Why partner with us?
TEDxIITHyderabad connects individuals and organizations who share the common goal of fostering remarkable thinking and powerful ideas, in areas of relevance to the local community. We offer you an opportunity to partner with us and promote ideas to a worldwide audience that can change the world for the better, in unorthodox ways. Through an individually tailored TEDx partnership, we look forward to welcoming you into our global community of leaders. We unearth world-changing ideas, thereby creating immersive experiences with the community.
As a TEDx partner, you have:
  • Partnership with an event of global recognition with exposure to a diverse populace

  • Ability to engage in authentic dialogue with a passionate, open-minded, and spirited audience

  • Generous media coverage ensures armation and visibility of your brand at an unprecedented scale

  • Great publicity, through our online platforms

  • Recognition in the programs and main events
We also intend to work with each partner individually to ensure impactful events that shape the experiences of the community in myriad ways. We hope that you become a part of our mission to work with curious minds and spread groundbreaking ideas by partnering with TEDxIITHyderabad. We rely on partners like you for a great event.
